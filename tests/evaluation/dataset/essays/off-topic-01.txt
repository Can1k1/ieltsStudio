Climate change is one of the most serious problems facing the world today. Ice caps are melting and sea levels are rising. Governments must invest in renewable energy and reduce carbon emissions from factories and vehicles.

Solar power has become cheaper in many countries. Wind farms are expanding along coastlines. However, some politicians still support coal mining because it provides jobs in their regions. This short-term thinking damages the environment.

Individuals can also help by recycling, using public transport, and eating less meat. Education campaigns have encouraged young people to protest for stronger environmental laws.

In conclusion, protecting the planet requires cooperation between citizens, businesses, and international organisations. If we fail to act now, future generations will suffer severe consequences.
